\section{Introduction}

The systems programming language Rust has been on the rising edge of popularity \cite{stack_overflow_stack_2022} and distribution. Organizations like Discord \cite{jesse_howarth_why_2020}, Chromium \cite{dana_jansens_supporting_2023} or the Linux Kernel Organization \cite{noauthor_rust_nodate} are embracing the advantages of Rust and steadily integrating the language into their codebase.
